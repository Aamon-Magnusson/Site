About

2022-11-16 :: Aamon

Hello,

I’m Aamon.

I work as a cyber security analyst. Within that I work as a pentester, SOC analyst, and what ever else I’m given.

I’ve also gained an interest into Gnu/Linux (That is that last time I will add the Gnu on this site), and open source software. I’ve grown to love the customization, and terminal use, that much FOSS software provides. With that I use Neovim, and DWM as my main preferred pieces of software. (I will likely try to write something about my preferred workflow)

I am also familiar with Windows. (Thanks Microsoft for not making that optional :|) Powertoys is an amazing collection of software to make Windows that much more usable. (Also it’s FOSS :))

Outside of tech, I love riding, and working on bikes. I currently own 5 bikes, ranging from a fixed gear, to a IBIS Ripmo AF (Full suspension mountain bike).

On this site I will write about whatever I decide to. Some may be guides for things that I know, or that I just learned. This will help me ingrain the skills I have and learn. Others may be recipes that I want to keep a hold of. But in total this is largely to get me to write, which is far from one of my highlighted skills. I tend to think of myself as a technical person, and this may help me branch out of that.

I doubt many will get even here, but thanks for reading, and please reach out to give any guidance or recommendations.
